About the Department

The Department of Computer Applications is established at MBITS with the Master of Computer Application (MCA) program in August 2023. The department aims at inculcating advanced knowledge and practical skills in students in the field of computer science and information technology. Well qualified and experienced faculty members and technical staff members are assets of our department. Students are being trained to improve their technical skills as well as soft skills which enable them to become industry-ready professionals. Students are encouraged to participate in various technical and extra-curricular programs conducted within and outside college. Smart classrooms and laboratory facilities are highly equipped with licensed software.

Vision

To be a globally recognized center of excellence in computer applications and research, empowering students with the knowledge and skills to excel in the ever-evolving digital world and preparing students for successful careers in the field of computer science and information technology.

Mission

To equip students with advanced knowledge and practical skills in computer science and information technology, enabling them to excel in the dynamic digital landscape and contribute effectively to the IT industry and society. Also, to foster a culture of research, ethical computing, and lifelong learning, preparing graduates to be innovative, responsible, and adaptable professionals in the field
