About College

MBITS Engineering College was established in 2009 and is renowned for its exceptional instruction and steadfast dedication to fostering students' engineering dreams. Its accreditation by the renowned National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC), an independent body operating under the auspices of the University Grants Commission (UGC) in India, further emphasises the institution's distinguished standing. The MBITS Engineering College fosters an environment where students can thrive and fulfil their engineering aspirations through an unwavering commitment to academic achievement. Due to its extensive history and accreditation, the college is unquestionably one of the best options for students looking for a top-notch engineering education.

About The Department

The Department of Computer Applications is established at MBITS with the Master of Computer Application (MCA) programme in August 2023. The department aims at inculcating advanced knowledge and practical skills in students in the field of computer science and information technology. Well qualified and experienced faculty members and technical staff members are assets of our department. Students are being trained to improve their technical skills as well as soft skills which enable them to become industry-ready professionals. Students are encouraged to participate in various technical and extra-curricular programmes conducted within and outside college. Smart classrooms and laboratory facilities are highly equipped with licensed software.

Facility of College

The infrastructure of MBITS is well-equipped to give its students a relaxing and supportive study environment. The institute features a central library, computer laboratories, conference rooms, a fitness centre, a store, an ATM, and a seminar hall in addition to a hostel for boys and girls. The hostel is roomy and equipped with everything you could possibly need, including cosy beds, private toilets, laundry rooms, and a mess area. The library is home to a sizable print and digital collection of books, journals, and magazines. The conference rooms have audio-visual equipment for holding seminars and workshops, and the computer labs are furnished with the newest models of computers and software. The store offers a variety of goods, including drinks, snacks, and stationery, and the fitness centre contains a choice of exercise equipment. The seminar hall is a spacious space that may be utilised for lectures, seminars, and workshops. It is also handy for students to take out cash from the ATM.
